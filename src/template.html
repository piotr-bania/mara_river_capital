Asymmetric investments sourced from developed mid-markets

Mara River is a Dallas-based investment firm launched in 2019.
Our mission is to capture the dynamic potential of small to midcap qeuities without the typical volatility.

We conduct original research at scale to identify high-potential investments in developed markets, applying our hedge fund background and cross-asset class risk management expertise to deliver asymmetric, uncorrelated returns with low volatility.

Richard Evans

Co-Managing Partner & CIO



Richard co-founded Mara River Capital in 2018. He was previously a senior analyst at King Street Capital, a ~$20 billion multi-strategy hedge fund which he joined in 2005. At King Street, Richard covered a wide range of industries and played a key role in building King Street’s European analyst team.

Prior to joining King Street, he worked as an analyst on the propriety trading desk at Nomura, having joined in 2003 from E&Y. Richard began his career in 1998 working for the restructuring division of Arthur Andersen (acquired by EY), where his work included the direct supervision of operating businesses. He is a Chartered Accountant and holds a BSc (Hons) Accounting & Finance 1st Class from the University of Warwick.
